Eli Kay-Oliphant

Eli Kay Oliphant is an American lawyer who rose to prominence as the spouse of Marina Squerciati, a Hollywood actress best known for her role as Kim Burgess in the TV series Chicago PD and for her participation in the fifth season of Gossip Girl.

Details On Her Net Worth And Career

Eli Kay-Oliphant is believed to have a net worth of about $500,000. His worth came from his work as a lawyer. His wife, on the other hand, has a net worth of roughly $1 million.

Eli’s occupation is that of a lawyer. Contractual disputes, commercial fraud, and other corporate torts are the center of his practice.

He began his work with Paul Hastings as a summer associate. Oliphant was an associate at O’Melveeny & Myers LLP in 2004. He began working as a law clerk for the Honorable W. Eugene Davis of the Fifth Circuit Court of Appeals a few years later. He has also worked for Latham & Watkins and Kobre & Kim LLP as an associate.
