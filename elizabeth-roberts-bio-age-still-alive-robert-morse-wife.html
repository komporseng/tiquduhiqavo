Meet Elizabeth Roberts, Robert Morse’s Wife

Moreover, now deceased Robert also had brought in a couple of kids from his previous marriage.

Before Elizabeth, the That’s Life actor was married to actress Carole D’Andrea. They were married in 1961 and had remained so for 20 years before divorcing in 1981.

So it began, his career, with an uncredited role in The Proud and Profane in 1956. Thereon, he was cast as Barnaby Tucker in the original Broadway production of Thornton Wilder’s The Matchmaker. This is what later on marked the launching of his career.
