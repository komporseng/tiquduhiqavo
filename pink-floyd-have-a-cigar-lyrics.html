Pink Floyd Have a Cigar Lyrics

“Have a Cigar Lyrics” is a song by the British rock band Pink Floyd. Bassist Roger Waters wrote the song and features lead vocals by English folk singer Roy Harper. The lyrics were directed at music industry executives and were meant to satirize their insincerity and greed.

About

Pink Floyd is a British rock band that formed in London in 1965. The band’s original lineup consisted of Syd Barrett (guitar, vocals), Roger Waters (bass, vocals), Richard Wright (keyboards, vocals), Nick Mason (drums), and later, David Gilmour (guitar, vocals).

Pink Floyd’s music is known for its elaborate instrumental arrangements, philosophical lyrics, and innovative use of sound effects and recording technology. The band’s early music was heavily influenced by blues, psychedelic rock, and experimental avant-garde music, but their sound evolved to incorporate elements of progressive rock, hard rock, and even jazz.

Pink Floyd became one of the most commercially successful and critically acclaimed bands of the 1970s, with landmark albums like “The Dark Side of the Moon”, “Wish You Were Here”, and “Animals”. These albums are considered classics of rock music and feature some of the band’s most famous songs, including “Money”, “Comfortably Numb”, and “Another Brick in the Wall”.

Although the band underwent many personnel changes throughout their career, they continued to release successful albums until their final studio album, “The Endless River” in 2014. Pink Floyd’s legacy as one of the greatest rock bands of all time continues to influence and inspire musicians around the world today.
