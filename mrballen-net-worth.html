MrBallen Net Worth

MrBallen is an American YouTuber who has an estimated net worth of $1.4 million. MrBallen is the online moniker of Jonathan B. Allen.

MrBallen is a former American Navy Seal who has built a large online following through his many YouTube channels. His content revolves around true crime cases and other mysteries that he covers and unveils to his followers.

What sets him apart from other YouTubers discussing similar topics is that he outlines his videos in a story form, and also because of his long-running joke of hating the like button.

His pleasant personality and rapport with his audience led him to amass over 7.02 million subscribers on his main channel. Alongside his successful main channel, he has three other channels of varying success.

MrBallen Net Worth In 2023

MrBallen net worth currently stands at $1.4 million. 

MrBallen built a successful true crime channel. Despite joining YouTube on March 25, 2015, Allen would not post his first video until March 30, 2020. His channel saw success almost immediately as he reached a million subscribers within the year on November 13, 2020. 

He would reach 2 million subscribers within six months on March 26, 2021, and 3 million subscribers in another two months on June 20, 2021. Then, another four months in, on September 16, 2021, he reached 4 million subscribers and closed the year out, reaching 5 million subscribers on May 21, 2022. 

His success is reflected monetarily and not only in subscriber milestones, as the tabulation website SocialBlade states that Allen makes anywhere between $6,900 to $110,400 per month from his channel and between $82,800 to $1.3 million per year. 

Allen has different streams of income. As stated above, he has four channels besides his main MrBallen channel. 

Of these three channels, the shorts and podcast ones are also very successful, with the former having over 2.24 million subscribers while the latter possesses over 234K subscribers.

How Much Money Is MrBallen Making From His Podcast Channel?

He has released over 90 episodes of the podcast, which viewers can listen to for free if they have an Amazon Prime subscription. 

The deal that MrBallen made with Amazon Prime is a 3-year exclusive deal but only concerns his podcast. This means the deal won't affect his video content on YouTube.
