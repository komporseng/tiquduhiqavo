How did Tai Emery start his Professional Career?

Sam Roberts started working in radio in 2002 at WERW. He began working as an intern in 2005 for the Opie and Anthony radio program on the XM Satellite Radio channel in New York. In 2006, he was appointed an assistant producer for the program.

He started producing it in January 2010. Roberts took over as presenter of the Opie and Anthony Radio Show in April 2011. He was also given the title of executive director in February 2014.

In June 2015, he started presenting the Sam Roberts Show, and he did so until October 2016. With East Side Dave, he co-hosted the Sirius XM program “Special Delivery.” Before becoming a weekly live show, it aired on Saturday evenings as a pre-recorded program. On SiriusXM Hits 1, he also presented an overnight Top 40 music program. It went on for four years.

Together with comedian Jim Norton, he presently co-hosts the morning program “Jim Norton and Sam Roberts.” On October 4, 2016, it made its premiere on the Opie Radio channel of SiriusXM (now called Faction Talk).

He runs a wrestling podcast in addition to hosting radio programs. Sam Roberts’ Wrestling program, his program, debuted in October 2014. After that, it adopted the moniker Notsam Wrestling.

He warmly greeted WWE superstars like Corey Graves, Paul Heyman, and Brock Lesnar. The year after, he shared the stage with Kevin Owens for a live performance before SummerSlam. Before SummerSlam in 2017, he co-hosted a show with Mark Henry.

The Swerve, a program hosted by Vince Russo, featured him in a two-part episode. He participated in a WWE roundtable discussion at Roadblock: End of the Line in December 2016. Since then, he has taken part in WWE Network events.

In 2017, he presided over WWE Talking Smack. He also presented the final Talking Smack episode in 2018. He has also served as a commentator for the WWE Main Event. Sam Roberts appeared as a special commentator on NXT alongside Tom Phillips.

While working as a DJ, he recorded a voiceover for Grand Theft Auto: Vice City Stories. He played a minor part in the movie Top Five as well. On a Bar Rescue episode, he and Jim Norton played recon agents.
