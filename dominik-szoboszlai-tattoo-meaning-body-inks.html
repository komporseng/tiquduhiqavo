As suggested by CFW Sports, Hungarian national player Dominik Szoboszlai is a Catholic. However, the professional footballer rarely mentions his religion to the media and the public.

Catholicism is the most commonly practised religion in Hungary. Over 54 per cent of the overall population identifies as Catholics.

Regarding his origin, Szoboszlai originated from Szekesfehervar. The place is an industrial city near Budapest.
