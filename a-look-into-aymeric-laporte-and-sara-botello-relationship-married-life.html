A Look Into Aymeric Laporte And Sara Botello Relationship: Married Life

Aymeric Laporte is an international soccer player. He married his long-term partner Sara Botello in June 2023. The famous defender only invited two colleagues from his club Manchester City to a lavish wedding in Spain attended by intimate friends and family. Among them was City striker Erling Haaland, who was joined by his girlfriend, Isabel Johansen, to bless the newlyweds.

Speaking of them, Aymeric and Sara have a son who was born out of wedlock. While he lives in England, his wife divides her time between Manchester and Bilbao, her homeland. Aymeric Laporte is a professional Spanish soccer player who plays left defender for the Spanish national team. Laporte, who was born in Agen, France, to a Basque family, first represented France in multiple competitions for different age categories before electing to play for Spain. Starting in 2021, he appeared in 22 games for Spain, scoring one goal in each tournament.

Aymeric is one of Europe’s most accomplished soccer defenders outside of his national team career. He was a member of the Atletico Bilbao side that won the Supercopa de Espana in 2015, the club’s first trophy in 31 years. He also won five Premier League championships and a UEFA Champions League with Manchester City.

Who Is Aymeric’s Wife’s Sara Botello?

Aymeric Laporte, a Spanish soccer defender, is married to his long-term lover, Sara Botello. After years of courting, the couple married on June 25, 2023. The French-born renowned athlete first visited Spain in 2010. When Aymeric first met Sara Botello in 2016, he was already a main defender for Atletico Bilbao. Sara, a soccer fan from Bilbao, recognized Aymeric and was instantly taken with him when he approached her.

Sara Botello, who was born and reared in Bilbao, is a trained dancer who specializes in ballet and modern dance. She formerly worked as a cheerleader for the basketball team Bilbao Basket, in addition to performing in dance exhibitions in her hometown. Laporte and Sara were upfront about their relationship and posted images of themselves on social media.

After almost six years of marriage, the couple had their first child, Lucay, in August 2022. Aymeric later announced his arrival on Instagram, writing, “There’s a new Cityzen in town: LUCAY, Mom, dad, and baby are all doing well, we are both very proud and thankful for this incredible moment.” Thank you for your kind words.”

Laporte’s Younger Brother Is a Professional Soccer Player as Well

Aymeric Laporte was born on May 27, 1994, to Lionel and Agnes Laporte. His parents also had a sister, Alexia, and a younger brother, Leo Laporte. Aymeric Laporte’s younger brother, born in 2004, is also a professional soccer player. Leo, unlike his elder brother, plays as a striker and on occasion as a winger. Leo began his professional career at the age of 16 in 2020. In October 2018, he earned his first professional deal with Deportivo Alaves after ending his youth career.

Following the agreement, he said on Instagram, “I am happy to announce that I have signed my first professional contract at @deportivoalaves.” “I’d like to thank my parents, brothers and sisters, family, and loved ones for sticking with me this far.” However, he is no longer a member of the club. The great soccer player’s younger brother is on Instagram and has over 7.5k followers as of this writing.
