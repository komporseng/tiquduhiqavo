TONI BRAXTONS KIDS ARE GROWING UP FAST

Toni Braxton’s kids are growing fast! In fact, the singer’s teenage sons Diezel and Denim spent the New Year’s without their mom.

Toni took to her Twitter page on the holiday to share a slideshow of her sons. She captioned of the video, “My first New Years without my boys they’re hanging out in Vegas! They’re growing up! HAPPY NEW YEARS. Have fun, Mom loves you…ALWAYS!”
