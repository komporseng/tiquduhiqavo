Steven Crowder Wife Hilary Crowder Is An Interior Designer

Steven Crowder wife Hilary Crowder is an interior designer and former sales manager. Steven got married to Hilary in August 2012. 

Steven cannot avoid the headlines because of his political comments and often online controversies. 

Throughout the years, he has faced many accusations and has been at the center of several controversies.

While he speaks about maintaining openness, his recent revelation about a contract offer from The Daily Wire might have just run him into a feud with fellow commentator Ben Shapiro.

The Louder with Crowder host revealed details about a $50 million contract offer from The Daily Wire and rejected it by titling it as a slave contract, as reported by Forbes.

This conflict has surfaced openly on the internet and is creating chaotic headlines.

He is again in the limelight, and many people are dwelling further into the ongoing fiery exchanges. Amid this, the attention has also fallen on the personal life of the YouTuber as many individuals have taken an interest in his family and love life.

Steven Crowder Wife Hilary Crowder

Steven Crowder is married to Hilary Crowder, and the couple has been together for over a decade.

Hilary is 35 years old and works as an interior designer. Before getting into the field of internal decoration, she worked as a sales manager, as reported by The Sun.

She graduated from Calvin College in Michigan with a political science degree at 23 in 2010. Although she completed her academics in politics, she decided to change her career path and ventured into design.

As she has opted to live a private life away from the online hassle and exposure, nothing much about her workplace and recent job whereabouts are familiar. She currently lives in Dallas, Texas, with her husband.

She suffers from Guillian Barre Syndrome (GBS), a medical condition where the nerves are damaged and causing muscle weakness.

This situation can even lead to paralysis. The Louder With Crowder podcast host revealed the news by sharing her pictures in the hospital on Instagram in 2020.

Steven Crowder And Hilary Crowder Relationship Timeline

Steven Crowder and his wife Hilary's relationship started blooming towards the beginning of the 2010s.

They reportedly met in 2011 and dated for around a year before the man proposed to his girlfriend in 2012. They got engaged in March 2012.

Despite spending more than a decade together, neither of them had opened up about how they met for the first time or when they started going out. 

Around five months after their engagement, Steven and Hilary tied the knot in August 2012. They exchanged marriage vows at the grand wedding function and have been together ever since. They celebrated their 10th anniversary in 2022 and are living a blissful romance.

The couple was expecting their first child in 2020, but the happiness ended shortly after as Hilary suffered a miscarriage. Around a year later, the two announced happy news about expecting twins.

In August 2021, the Crowder couple became the parents of two kids, a son named Magnus and a daughter named Charlotte. They are now a sweet family of four people.

Steven often shares pictures of his wife and family on his Instagram account under the handle @louderwithcrowder. It s a verified account and has over 1.5 million followers.

Mother Francine Crowder

Francine Crowder is the mother of American political commentator Steven.
