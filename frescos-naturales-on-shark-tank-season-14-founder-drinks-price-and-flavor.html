Frescos Naturales on Shark Tank season 14, founder, drinks price and flavor

The recent episode of Shark Tank Season 14 saw a founder of the beverage brand, Frescos Naturales which came to the business reality show to represent his unique business idea

For those who are unversed, let us tell you, the founder of the Frescos Naturales, a beverage brand which believes to have the “traditional flavor of aguas frescas with a modern twist of bubbles,” came to the Episode 12 of the show.

The founder, Juan Ignacio Stewart came to the stage to ask the panel of 5 Sharks if they wanted to fund his venture. The founder was quite excited as he announced on Instagram that he will be appearing on Season 14 of the Show.

Frescos Naturales on Shark Tank season 14, founder, drinks price, flavor

Frescos Naturales

For those who are unversed, let us tell you, the brand is a Latino-owned beverage company which specializes in making excellent and refreshing Aguas Frescas.

The brand allegedly creates drinks that are of highest quality ingredients along with a trace amount of sugar.

Juan Stewart, who’s the founder of the brand, grew up in La Antigua, Guatemala and migrated to Boulder, Colorado when he was 17.

Advertisement

The founder of the company claims that he always loved Aguas Frescas and grew up drinking them prepared from fruits such as tamarind, hibiscus, pineapple among many more.

The idea to bottle these beverages and sell them occurred to him when he was having a meal with his son in 2018. At the time, he started selling Rosa de Jamaica (hibiscus) at the local farmers’ markets.

Soon enough, he saw the success of the Rosa de Jamaica which led him to create many new flavors and as a result, he founded Frescos Naturales.

The brand offers fruit drinks such as Mango, Guayaba, Piña, Maracuya, Tamarindo, and Rosa de Jamaica among many more.

The company offers drinks at Boulder area cafe’s, restaurants and delis as well as online on the website of drinkfrescos.com.

Advertisement

The customer of the brand can purchase a mixed 6-pack sample of all flavors as well as a single flavor 6-pack.

Price

The prices of these 6-pack products will cost you around $19.99. The founder reveals that he directly sells these products to consumers as well as to tiny mom-and-pop cafes and businesses all around Colorado.

Juan came to the Shark Tank to represent his unique business venture to a panel of 5 Sharks to ask them to join in his venture.

Let us remind you, Episode 12 of Shark Tank Season 14 will air at around 08:00 P.M. Eastern Standard Time on ABC on Friday, January 20, 2023.
