Likewise, he followed it up with the single ‘Racks’, which came out on November 9, 2017. Similarly, ‘Now’ was put out in January 2018 which featured Lil Skies.

The two had been communicating for a while through social media and wanted to work together. Moreover, ‘Now’ was an even bigger hit than ‘Pull Up’. Therefore, it quickly gained 7 million playbacks on SoundCloud.

Further, the music video for the song was put out on YouTube in October. And, in less than just two weeks, it received more than a million views. This rapper has developed close friendships with Lil Skies and rapper, Landon Cube.
