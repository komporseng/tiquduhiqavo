What Driver Does Tiger Woods Use?

Few players have turned our attention and speculation more concerning golf equipment than Tiger Woods. The driver, however, is arguably the most important equipment in his bag. So, in this feature, we'll go through everything in detail, ranging from Tiger Woods's driver to the technical specs, the reason for choosing this setup, how that affects his performance and much more. 

Make, Model, and Specifications

With Tiger Woods' driver charting back over multiple platforms through years past, he has been linked to several memorable models. He has mainly gamed the TaylorMade M2 and TaylorMade Stealth Plus drivers.

The TaylorMade M2 Driver  is further enhanced by a multi-material construction that combines a Speed Pocket, Geocoustic Internal Geometry, and Carbon crown. It also pairs up with a 9-degree loft set and comes spaced at 45.75 inches long, offering Woods the perfect balance of distance and forgiveness out on the course.

Meanwhile, the TaylorMade Stealth Plus driver features an adjustable sole weight, a 9-degree loft, and a 45.75-inch length shaft, providing Woods with added control and distance.

What Compelled Tiger Woods to Choose TaylorMade

Tiger has chosen the TaylorMade drivers because of their state-of-the-art technology, which will provide him with much longer, higher-flying, and accurate tee-balls, something he desires to compete at a high level. That includes the adjustability of Woods' new drivers, which he uses to dial in his ball flight and gain its full performance advantage when playing a course. As part of that, the brand's reputation for producing tour-proven equipment would also surely have weighed in Woods' decision to switch TaylorMade drivers.

Achievement and Performance-Based Contribution

TaylorMade played a crucial role in the success of Tiger Woods on the golf course. The forgiveness, distance, and control combo has given Woods the confidence to take on any hole design while getting his launch conditions right, which should lead to more accuracy and length from tee to green. It has helped Woods stay at the top of his game as golf becomes more and more specialized.

Tailoring and customization

Tiger Woods is renowned for having a number of intricate tweaks made to his drivers over the years so that they suit him. Specifically, he has altered the sole weight and hosel settings of his TaylorMade driver to produce specific types of ball flight with a touch more draw bias with a bit of upright lie angles-stressing just how precise needs for shot shape are becoming.

Overall, Tiger Woods' choice of drivers has fueled intense gossip and intrigue, especially given his career in the sport's hallowed halls.
