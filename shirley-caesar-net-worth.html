Shirley Caesar’s Gospel Music Career and Net Worth

Shirley Caesar is an American gospel music singer-songwriter from Durham, North Carolina. She began singing gospel music as a child and recorded her first tracks at age eleven. After performing with the popular gospel group The Caravans for eight years, she pursued a solo career. Caesar graduated from Shaw University with a degree in Business Administration and attended the Divinity School at Duke University. She has released 20 solo albums and won seven Dove Awards and two Black Gospel Awards. Her net worth is estimated to be $16 million dollars. In addition to her music career, she is one of the pastors at Mount Calvary Word of Faith Church in Raleigh, North Carolina.
