How did Kim Min-kyu start his Professional Career?

Kim’s acting career began in 2013 when he was cast in a minor role in the Mnet drama Monstar. Kim has appeared in several TV shows, including Signal (2016), Because This Is My First Life (2017), The Rich Son (2018), and Perfume (2019). Kim rose to prominence as Lee Kyung in Queen: Love and War (2019-20), a historical drama set during the Joseon period.

In 2020, Kim portrayed Kang Ji-Wook in the SBS drama Backstreet Rookie. Kim starred in So I Married the Anti-fan, which premiered on Naver TV in April 2021. In the JTBC drama Snowdrop, he portrayed Joo Gyeok-chan, a cold-hearted North Korean agent. Kim appeared as chief secretary Cha Sung-hoon in the SBS romantic comedy-drama Business Proposal in 2022.
